Palawan, Philippines

About Palawan

Palawan, often referred to as the Philippines' Last Frontier, is a paradise for nature lovers. It is known for its crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and lush jungle. The island is home to some of the world's most beautiful natural wonders, including the Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, Tubbataha Reefs Natural Park, and El Nido's stunning limestone cliffs.
