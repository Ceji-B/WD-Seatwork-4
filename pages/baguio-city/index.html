"Baguio City: Where the Pines Whisper and the Flowers Smile!"

TRAVI | TRAVELS

June 29, 2024

Escape to the "Summer Capital of the Philippines" and experience the cool, refreshing climate of Baguio City. Nestled in the mountains of Northern Luzon, Baguio offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and historical landmarks. Stroll through lush pine forests, explore vibrant flower gardens, and immerse yourself in the city's vibrant arts scene. Book your Baguio adventure today and create unforgettable memories in this enchanting highland paradise!

Immerse yourself in the heart of Baguio City at Burnham Park. This iconic park, designed by American architect Daniel Burnham, is a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike. Take a leisurely boat ride on the serene man-made lake, have a picnic on the expansive lawns, or enjoy a day of fun with various outdoor activities. With its beautiful gardens, playgrounds, and recreational facilities, Burnham Park is the perfect place to unwind and soak in the beauty of Baguio.


For breathtaking vistas and a taste of local culture, Mines View Park is a must-visit. Located on a scenic promontory, this park offers stunning panoramic views of the Cordillera Mountains and the historic mining town of Itogon. Capture the perfect photo against the majestic backdrop, shop for unique souvenirs, and try on traditional Igorot attire for a memorable experience. Mines View Park is a wonderful spot to appreciate the natural beauty and rich heritage of Baguio City.


Once a U.S. military recreational facility, Camp John Hay has transformed into Baguio City's premier destination for luxury and adventure. Nestled amidst towering pine trees and meticulously landscaped gardens, Camp John Hay offers a range of activities for all ages. Enjoy a round of golf on the world-class course, embark on scenic hiking trails, or get your adrenaline pumping with thrilling zip-line adventures. With luxurious accommodations, gourmet dining, and a tranquil environment, Camp John Hay provides the perfect blend of relaxation and excitement in Baguio City.
